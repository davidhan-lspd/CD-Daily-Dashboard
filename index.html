Analysis

  • Unsystematic risk add-ons total +3.5pp: size premium +2.0% (Small cap ($500M-$2B)) and company-specific risk premium +1.5%. Without these, textbook CAPM understates LSPD's true cost of capital by ~3.5pp.
  • Beta of 1.81 is elevated vs market (1.0), adding ~4.5pp to systematic risk premium vs an average-risk firm.
  • Peer WACC range: Wix 8.59% (lowest cost of capital) to Shopify 19.74% (highest). Peer median: 15.61%.
  • Each 1pp increase in WACC roughly translates to ~10-15% reduction in DCF-implied equity value for high-growth SaaS — monitor rate moves and changes to risk premiums accordingly.
  • Assumptions (ERP 5.5%, pre-tax debt 6.0%, tax 26%, CSRP 1.5%) are reasonable defaults but should be calibrated to specific deal contexts. CSRP especially is judgmental — Lightspeed's could go higher if you weight management transitions, SMB cyclicality, or Asia-Pac execution risk more heavily.
Modified CAPM: Cost of Equity = Rf + β × ERP + Size Premium + Company-Specific Risk Premium. Textbook CAPM assumes diversified investors so unsystematic risk drops out — but in practice, size premium and idiosyncratic risk are well-documented and routinely added.
